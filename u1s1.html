  5. The Trail Mix Calorie Counter gives the following information on its website. Use the pattern in the table to answer the questions.
    Grams of trail mix Calories
    50 220
    150 660
    300 1320
    500 2200
    1. Eddie eats 75 grams of trail mix. How many calories does he eat?

      2. \(\frac{50\;grams}{220\;calories}=\frac{75\;grams}{x\;calories}\)

      \(50x=16,500\)

      \(\frac{\displaystyle50x}{50}=\frac{\displaystyle16,500}{50}\)

      \(x=330\)

      Eddies eats 330 calories

    2. Claire eats trail mix containing 1000 calories. How many grams of trail mix does she eat?

      3. \(\frac{50\;grams}{220\;calories}=\frac{x\;grams}{1000\;calories}\)

      \(50,000=220x\)

      \(\frac{\displaystyle50,000}{220}=\frac{\displaystyle220x}{220}\)

      \(x=227\)

      Claire eats 227 grams
